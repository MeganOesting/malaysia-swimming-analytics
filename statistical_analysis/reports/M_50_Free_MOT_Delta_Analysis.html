  • 23.2% of 16→17 dropouts reappear: About one-fifth of swimmers who drop out at 17 return by 18.

  • MOT Consideration: Delta calculations may reflect 'survivor bias' - swimmers who maintain top-500 status consistently show different improvement patterns than those who drop out.

  • The 'Good But Not Great' Dilemma: Swimmers in the 1.5-2.0 z-score range (top 6.7%) face unique challenges - they're faster than most, but may be hitting their genetic/training ceiling.
