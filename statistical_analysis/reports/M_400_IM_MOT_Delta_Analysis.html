Interpretation

  • 61.9% of 15→16 dropouts reappear: Nearly one-third of swimmers who fall out of the top 500 at age 16 regain elite status by age 17 or 18.
  • 30.0% of 16→17 dropouts reappear: About one-fifth of swimmers who drop out at 17 return by 18.
  • Implications: Temporary setbacks (injury, training changes, motivation) are common, but most dropouts (~70%) do not return to top-500 status.
  • MOT Consideration: Delta calculations may reflect 'survivor bias' - swimmers who maintain top-500 status consistently show different improvement patterns than those who drop out.
